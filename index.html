Preferred partners

Universities we work closely with

Every partner is chosen for outcomes, not just prestige — real programmes, real support, real results for our students.

York St John University

Established as York Diocesan College and granted university status in 2006, York St John sits on a large urban campus with over 6,000 students across Art, Design & Computer Science, Education, and Health Sciences.

University of Worcester

A public research university dating back to 1946, awarding degrees since 1997 and granted full university status in 2005 — the only university serving Worcestershire and Herefordshire.

University of East London

Based across three campuses in Stratford and Docklands, UEL is home to more than 17,000 students from 135 countries, offering a genuinely global community.
